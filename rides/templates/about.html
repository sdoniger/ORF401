Welcome to Carpool Connect. We are a rideshare app that allows people to make connections with fellow travelers in order to split a ride from their origination to destinations. The goal of this app is to not only create a convenient travel option but also to encourage a more sustainable way to travel by car, as sharing a ride between multiple people lowers the carbon emissions per capita and can therefore have a significant impact in attempts to reduce global carbon emissions.
